Ice Recipe



How to make ice


Its pretty hard, get some water and put it in the freezer. Hopefully, you know how to get water (the faucet) and you have a freezer.
Be sure not to put your water in a sealed container, or you might break it. Remember, it takes time for the water to freeze so be patient.

Ingredients

  • Water

Recipe

  1. Find source of water
  2. Put water in container (un covered)
  3. Put water in freezer
  4. Wait a bit
  5. Consume with your drink (or soup) of choice

Ice is good with a lot of things. Try it with water first. No, not the water you just froze, thats ice now. I mean unfrozen water.
